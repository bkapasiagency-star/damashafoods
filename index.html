About Damasha Foods

The manufacturing foundation behind natural, trusted food brands

Damasha Foods LLP is a food manufacturing company headquartered in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, built on a simple premise: natural ingredients, treated with precision, produce better food. We design and operate our own extraction and processing lines, combining traditional cold pressing methods with modern process controls, hygiene standards, and quality systems.

We are the parent organization behind SATVARAS, our consumer-facing wellness brand. As a manufacturer, our focus stays upstream — sourcing, extraction, formulation, testing, and packaging — so our partners can build fast on a dependable supply.

Traditional Methods Modern Technology Natural Ingredients Customer-First

  1. FOUNDATION

    Built on a cold-press philosophy

    Damasha Foods began with a single wood-pressed oil line, rooted in the belief that heat and shortcuts strip food of what matters.

  2. EXPANSION

    Scaling processing capability

    Investment in extraction, filtration, and packaging lines allowed us to serve larger retail and institutional volumes without compromising process.

  3. BRAND

    Launch of SATVARAS

    Our consumer wellness brand launched to bring the same manufacturing standard directly to households, while Damasha remained focused on manufacturing.

  4. TODAY

    A manufacturing partner of scale

    Today, we manufacture for distributors, retail chains, exporters, and private label clients across categories — with quality systems built for global standards.
